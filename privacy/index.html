Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 16, 2026

Data Collection & Usage

Souti AAC runs locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or store personal data on remote servers.

  • Local Profile Data: Profile and settings data are stored locally on your device.
  • Custom Content: Images and audio recordings you add are stored locally on your device to provide your AAC experience.
  • Network & Analytics: Souti AAC does not send analytics, tracking, or personal data to any Souti server.

Purchases

Souti AAC is a one-time purchased app. We do not use recurring subscriptions or recurring billing.

Permissions

  • Camera access is used only when you choose to capture custom symbol images.
  • Microphone access is used only when you choose to record custom voice clips.
  • Photo library access is used only when you choose to upload or save custom images.

Data Protection

Souti AAC does not collect personal data and does not rely on backend servers for user content. Therefore, we do not sell, rent, trade, share, or transmit personal data to third parties.

Storage and Retention

Your symbols, images, recordings, and settings remain on your device. You can remove data directly in the app, and uninstalling the app removes local app data.

User Rights & Deletion

You have full control over your data and can delete symbols, media, and profile data directly from the app at any time.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Souti AAC complies with COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. For any privacy-related inquiries regarding a minor, please contact us with guardian verification.
