Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 16, 2026

Data Collection & Usage

Souti AAC is committed to protecting user privacy, especially for children. We collect minimal data necessary for the app's core functionality:

  • Account Data: Profile information is used for app settings and cross-device sync.
  • Custom Content: User-uploaded images and audio recordings are stored securely to provide your personalized AAC experience.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous diagnostics to help us fix bugs and improve performance.

Purchases

Souti AAC is a one-time purchased app. We do not use recurring subscriptions or recurring billing.

Permissions

  • Camera access is used only when you choose to capture custom symbol images.
  • Microphone access is used only when you choose to record custom voice clips.
  • Photo library access is used only when you choose to upload or save custom images.

Data Protection

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted.

Storage and Retention

Custom symbols, images, and voice recordings are stored in app-controlled storage and may be synced to secure cloud infrastructure when you enable account sync. Data remains until you delete it in-app or request account deletion.

User Rights & Deletion

You have full control over your data. Account deletion and privacy contact details will be published here once mailbox setup is complete.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Souti AAC complies with COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. For any privacy-related inquiries regarding a minor, please contact us with guardian verification.
